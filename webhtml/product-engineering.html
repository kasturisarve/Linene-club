Product Engineering

Apprise Your Product Development With Innovation


Building Profitable and Outstanding ProductsProducts engineered to fulfill future customer demand

The market for the product engineering services market is estimated to grow USD 1,003.12 Billion by 2021, at a CAGR of 8.2% from 2016 to 2021. While organizations are driven to constantly improve their product lifecycle elements they find it difficult to meet increasing customer expectations on quality, cost control, and fast-changing technologies. Therefore, demand for outsourced product engineering services is growing at an unprecedented pace today because the aim of these services is to help customers focus on their core business processes. Futurism Technologies helps its clients by delivering such state-of-the-art product engineering solutions. We work closely with our clients to design and deliver cutting-edge technology products. We employ our vast domain expertise and harness our partnerships with machine-driven engineering, electronics, and software vendors to deliver long-lasting product innovations.

Software Development

Software product development is an extensive process, and it includes various phases from programming, testing, documentation, and bug fixing. We support our clients through an entire lifespan of software product development, including:

  • Software Product ideation and prototype creation

  • Software Product Architecture

  • Product Testing and QA

  • Technical Support

Product Porting and Migration

    Product porting and migration is a continuous process across all business verticals. The reason is technologies are changing at a fast pace. Although most product companies engage their engineers to improve their software products, they prefer outsourcing tasks to Product Engineering companies like Futurism for greater value addition,

  • We specialize in migration, porting, and localization/ internationalization

  • Experience in varied business domains

  • Analysis of missing interfaces

  • Software or database porting from one porting system to another

Software Professional Services

    Moving running applications to the cloud, with clients on board may sound scary to you. At Futurism, we help you migrate your application successfully at any stage of the service lifecycle. We offer,

  • Pre-sales consulting for scope identification and business goal setting

  • Customization as per the client requirement and future scope

  • Consulting

  • Integration of other applications

Software SAAS Solutions

    Software SAAS solutions are the core of IT infrastructure nowadays. These solutions allow businesses to efficiently manage, purchase, and deploy software as per their requirement. We offer,

  • Combination of hybrid, SAAS, or on-premise models to suit client application, technology, business, and security needs

  • SaaS solutions that integrate seamlessly

  • Upgradation support for future modifications

  • High Availability

Enterprise Platform

    Most businesses depend on various types of paid or free enterprise platforms to streamline their business, but still, struggle to serve their clients efficiently. Futurism’s Enterprise E-Commerce Portal – Cogent Web Apps allows businesses to integrate their business functions with Microsoft ERP solutions such as GP, Navision, SAP, Dynamics, etc. It supports a variety of business models including,

  • B2B

  • B2C

  • Direct to Consumer

  • Consumer to Consumer

Software Support and Maintenance Services

  • Customer support
